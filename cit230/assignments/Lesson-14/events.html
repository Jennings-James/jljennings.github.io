Cycle the Continental Divide

SEPTEMBER 20-23, 2018

Cycle the Continental Divide annual event. We have 5 different classes including 2 youth classes and 3 adult classes. The pro class spans the full 4 days of the event and covers 250 miles and about 5000 ft of elevation change. The intermediate class is 2 days and 78 miles and about 1800 ft of elevation. The amateur class is 1 day and covers 45 miles and about 800 ft of elevation. Youth riders can choose from amateur which covers 20 miles in the foot hills with little elevation change. Or advanced which is the same as the adult amateur course.

Sign up before August 15 and recive a free event t-shirt
