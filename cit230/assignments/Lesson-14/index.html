UPCOMING EVENT

Cycle the Continental Divide annual event. We have 5 different classes including 2 youth classes and 3 adult classes. The pro class spans the full 4 days of the event and covers 250 miles and about 5000 ft of elevation change. The intermediate class is 2 days and 78 miles and about 1800 ft of elevation. The amateur class is 1 day and covers 45 miles and about 800 ft of elevation. Youth riders can choose from amateur which covers 20 miles in the foot hills with little elevation change. Or advanced which is the same as the adult amateur course. Sign up early and get an event t-shirt.

LOCAL MAN COMPETS IN MAJOR RACE

On June 29th thru the 31st the city of San Diego California held its annual Bike the Beach event. The courses take riders on a grand tour of the area starting on Point Loma near the Cabrillo National Monument. The route winds its way past the San Diego Zoo, then on to El Cajon before heading north to Escondido. After reaching Oceanside the route turns south and heads back to San Diego. The event lasts three days and is very fast paced with many twists and turns. Bozeman’s own “Fast” Freddy Flintstone finished a very impressive 3rd against the mostly pro field.
